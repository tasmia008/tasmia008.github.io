## About Me

I am Tasmia Tahmida Jidney, a Machine Learning researcher working at the intersection of medical imaging, self-supervised learning, and vision-language models. My current research, supervised by Md. Ashikuzzaman, focuses on physics-informed pipelines for ultrasound image analysis and on projection-layer design choices for medical VLMs. I previously worked as a Research Collaborator at the Center for Computational & Data Sciences (CCDS), Independent University Bangladesh (IUB), under Dr. Ashraful Islam, and earlier as a Research Assistant under Dr. Sajedul Talukder (UTEP) at Pioneer Alpha. I am broadly interested in deep learning, medical image understanding, JEPA/DINO-style self-supervised pretraining, and parameter-efficient adaptation of large foundation models.

I am open to collaborative opportunities that align with my research goals and passions. If you have any inquiries or wish to discuss potential collaborations, please feel free to reach out. You can contact me via email at **Personal:** [tasmiatahmida.8@gmail.com](mailto:tasmiatahmida.8@gmail.com).
